About PersonaTrack

PersonaTrack is a digital profiling system designed specifically for barangay officials in the Philippines. It helps streamline resident data collection, updates, and analysis, making it easier to provide community services, track demographics, and ensure safety. Our platform prioritizes data privacy and security, complying with local regulations.

Whether you're handling census data, emergency responses, or community programs, PersonaTrack simplifies the process with intuitive features.

Key Features

  • Secure Data Storage

    Encrypt and store resident profiles safely in the cloud, accessible only to authorized users.

  • User-Friendly Interface

    Easy-to-navigate dashboards for adding, editing, and searching profiles on any device.

  • Advanced Reporting

    Generate customizable reports on demographics, health stats, and more for better decision-making.

  • Real-Time Updates

    Receive notifications for profile changes and integrate with barangay services for seamless operations.
